## Key Results to Cite

If referencing specific findings from this work:

- **Tsirelson Ridge achievement**: McRae et al. (2025) demonstrated |S|=2.794 (98.8% of Tsirelson bound) in classical Kuramoto oscillators
- **RUT Plateau robustness**: Violations persist across noise levels σ=0.0–0.2 (McRae et al., 2025)
- **Forgetfulness boundary**: Classical behavior emerges below PLI ≈ 0.85 (McRae et al., 2025)
