# Paper 3 - Mission 3: Loop Charge & Braiding Dynamics (E341–E380)

## Purpose
Classify loop charge behavior (Φ) and identify braided or circulating echo
regimes that cannot arise in two-oscillator systems.

Mission 3 contains two scientific components:
- **Part 3A**: Loop-Charge (Φ) Dynamics
- **Part 3B**: Braiding & Phase-Order Dynamics

These are analyzed separately in the paper, though executed together for computational efficiency.
